Adjectivo + -mente = Advérbio
Add -mente to the feminine form of an adjective to make an adverb. For adjectives ending in -o: change to -a first, then add -mente. For adjectives ending in -e or a consonant: add -mente directly. Adjectives with accents drop the accent.
