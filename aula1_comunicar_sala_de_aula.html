Grammar Note — Obrigado/a

The ending changes to match the speaker's gender, not the listener's.
Man says: Obrigado · Woman says: Obrigada
The superlative -íssimo/a intensifies: Obrigadíssimo/a = extremely grateful.

Grammar Note — Como se diz?

Diz-se is the reflexive construction of dizer (to say), used impersonally. Literally: "One says it thus." The expression Diz-se assim! = "That's how you say it!"
