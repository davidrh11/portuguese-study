📅 Plano de Estudo Diário
Tap to expand — recommended daily routine
▼
☀️ Manhã 15 min
Desafio Drill
Open the most recent lesson guide. Go to the Desafio tab. Speak every answer aloud before revealing. Self-assess honestly. Full deck.
🚶 A Caminhar 20–40 min
Podcast
Rotate Ep. 1 → 2 → 3. Don't just listen — shadow the speaker. Say every Portuguese sentence aloud a half-second behind the audio.
🌙 Tarde/Noite 10 min
Flashcards — 1 Tópico
Pick one grammar guide. Do 10–15 cards. Rotate topics daily: possessivos → conectores → combinações → verbos. Never the same guide two days running.
📖 Pré-Aula 20 min
Review Current Lesson
Open both guides from the current week. Tabela tab only — scan all examples and speak them aloud. Prime your memory before class.
O princípio: Desafio and speaking aloud every day. Everything else is secondary. 15 focused minutes of active recall beats 90 minutes of passive reading. The bottleneck at A1 is production — the Desafio tab is the most important thing on this page.
